Let's Get Moving !



Benefits:

Did you know working out improves brain health, reduces the risk of disease, and improves your ability to do everday activties ?
Just 28% of Americans are Exercising Enough, according to the CDC.

Regular physical activity can favorably improve seven of the ten most common chronic diseases in the U.S., including heart disease and diabetes! Exercising also improves mental health by reducing anxiety, stress levels, and depression. Constant feeling of fatigue and not getting enough sleep? Exercising has been proven to increase energy levels and improve sleep.


Getting Started:
  1. Start Small: Starting your fitness journey doesn't have to be extreme. It can be as simple as starting each day with a 10-15 minute walk.
  2. Bring a friend: It's always easier to try things with a friend. Bringing a friend can make your fitness journey more enjoyable and help hold you accountable.
  3. Join a Local Gym Class: Gym Classes range from Zumba, Spin, or Yoga. These classes offer different workout options if weightlifting isn't for you.
  4. Stay Hydrated: Staying hydrated will help fuel your muscles and boost energy levels.
  5. Hold yourself accountable: It's so easy to give up once motivation runs out. Setting goals and holding yourself accountable will help you reach your fitness goals faster.
  6. Track your progress: On days when motivation is low. Seeing progress pictures or a drop in the scale can keep you going !

FREE available Resources:
  • Youtube: Youtube has thousands of free videos ranging from beginner to intermediate levels. Fitness options range from yoga, HIIT workouts, hiking, and so much more..
  • Your Local Hospital: Many local hospitals offer free zumba classes and other beginner fitness classes.
